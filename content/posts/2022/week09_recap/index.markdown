## Matchups Recap

### Paulinia Robots Gets Late Victory over Goncalves Miners, Relieves Them from 1st Place 

Paulinia Robots was clutch in prime time with 54.74 points on Sunday Night Football (39% of their total score) to pull off the comeback against Goncalves Miners 138.84 to 98.34. The late heroics were somewhat unexpected, since they were originally projected to score 41.28 points on Sunday night. Paulinia Robots was led by Patrick Mahomes (446 Pas Yds, 63 Rsh Yds, 1 2PT, 1 Pas TD/1 Int, 1 Rsh TD). Mahomes passed for 446 yards and added 34.14 points to the final tally. Paulinia Robots (7-2, 1,131.40 points) climbs into second place, while Goncalves Miners (7-2, 1,093.62 points) drops to fourth place.

 While Justin Fields led Paulinia Robots weeks (with 26.04 points), Mahomes has now been the team's top scorer in two of the last three matches.

 Paulinia Robots plays Campinas Giants (2-7, 906.48) in Week 10, while Goncalves Miners matches up against Limeira Dead Rabbits (4-5, 916.02).

### Powered by Stars, Sorocaba Steelers Gives the Business to Sorocaba Wild Mules 

Carried by the trio of Tyreek Hill, Travis Etienne, and Derrick Henry, Sorocaba Steelers humiliated Sorocaba Wild Mules 149.80 to 65.26. Combining for 77.40 points, that threesome alone surpassed the total of Sorocaba Wild Mules. Etienne scored two touchdowns, registering 26.60 points (109 Rsh Yds, 2 TD). Henry delivered two touchdowns to contribute 23.50 points (115 Rsh Yds, 2 TD), while Hill pitched in 27.30 points (143 Rec Yds, 1 TD). Sorocaba Steelers had their best score of the season, as well as the second-biggest margin of victory in the league. Sorocaba Steelers (7-2, 1,020.82 points) climbs into third place, while Sorocaba Wild Mules (1-8, 870.14 points) stays mired in last place.

 Sorocaba Wild Mules had one noteworthy player that missed out on some opportunities. Even though Kyle Pitts saw a total of seven targets, he only reeled in two catches.

 While JuJu Smith-Schuster led Sorocaba Steelers in Week 7 (with 25.40 points) and Derrick Henry in Week 8 (35.80 points), Hill has now been the team's top point-getter twice in the last four weeks. Also, with 30 touches this week for Travis Etienne, it means that his touches have now increased in three consecutive games.

 Sorocaba Steelers gets Amparo Bikers (3-6, 986.78) in Week 10, while Sorocaba Wild Mules matches up against London Knights (5-4, 799.00).

### Indaiatuba Riders Beats Amparo Bikers, Rises to 8th Place 

Indaiatuba Riders (4-5, 990.32 points) got the win over Amparo Bikers (3-6, 986.78 points) 123.10 to 100.36 in a matchup with three lead changes. Their top player was Davante Adams (146 Rec Yds, 2 TD), who had the 17th-highest player score for the season. Heading into Sunday, Indaiatuba Riders was out in front 24.00 to 8.00. That advantage slipped away after the early Sunday games and they trailed by 2.56. Indaiatuba Riders regained the lead during Sunday's late afternoon games, thanks to 11.00 points from Leonard Fournette. They would hold the lead until the end.

 There was one starter on Amparo Bikers that left points out on the field. Even though Amon-Ra St. Brown saw a total of nine targets, he reeled in just four catches.

 Since Adams was the top point-getter for Indaiatuba Riders, it means a different player has led the team in each of the last four weeks. With 22.10 points, Leonard Fournette paced the squad in Week 6, while Trevor Lawrence (18.70 points) was tops in Week 7 and Tony Pollard (33.70 points) in Week 8.

 Next week, Indaiatuba Riders gets Rio Claro Farmers (6-3, 1,046.30), while Amparo Bikers matches up against Sorocaba Steelers (7-2, 1,020.82).
