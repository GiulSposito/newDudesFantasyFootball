## League Highlights

Paulinia Robots, Sorocaba Steelers, and Rio Claro Farmers all got wins this week despite not starting a top-15 scoring QB.

Sorocaba Wild Mules was projected to win by the widest margin this week (12.45 points). Not only did they lose to Rio Claro Farmers, but they lost by a bigger margin (52.94 points) than they were projected to win by.

So far this season, teams have won only 33.3% of the time when starting the league's top-scoring QB of the week (1-2 overall).

Surprisingly, teams have won 75.0% of the time this season (6-2) when they fail to throw a Pass TD.

First-round draft picks averaged 17.24 points this week. Second-round players averaged 12.91, which was actually less than the third-rounders (14.42).

The 149.48 points scored by Rio Claro Farmers were the most this week and the third-highest overall in the league this season.

The average projected score was 110.12 points this week.

## Matchups Recap

### Paulinia Robots Wins Against Amparo Bikers to Grab 1st Place 

Paulinia Robots (3-0, 436.12 points) jumps into first place after beating Amparo Bikers (13th place, 1-2, 343.36 points) 144.14 to 114.02. D'Andre Swift (47 Rsh Yds, 60 Rec Yds, 1 TD) led Paulinia Robots with 23.70 points while Brandin Cooks (112 Rec Yds) scored 20.70. Paulinia Robots took a 12.70-point lead on Thursday and remained in front for the rest of the matchup. Still undefeated, Paulinia Robots is having a great start to the season.

 Miles Sanders probably deserved more touches for Amparo Bikers, as he put up a whopping 55 total yards on just five touches.

 Next week, Paulinia Robots gets Sorocaba Steelers (1-2, 271.68), while Amparo Bikers matches up with Rio Claro Farmers (1-2, 348.58).

### Rio Claro Farmers Drops the Hammer on Sorocaba Wild Mules, Claims 8th Place 

Rio Claro Farmers destroyed Sorocaba Wild Mules 149.48 to 96.54 while putting up the highest score this week. This beating was revenge for a loss to Sorocaba Wild Mules in their last meeting a season ago. Justin Herbert led Sorocaba Wild Mules with 30.84 points (281 Pas Yds, 1 2PT, 4 TD) while Chris Godwin racked up 19.60 (74 Rec Yds, 1 Rsh TD). Rio Claro Farmers (1-2, 348.58 points) climbs into eighth place and Sorocaba Wild Mules (1-2, 328.64 points) drops to 10th place.

 Singletary had plenty of opportunities for Sorocaba Wild Mules, but could only manage 26 total yards on 12 touches.

 Rio Claro Farmers gets Amparo Bikers (1-2, 343.36) in Week 4, while Sorocaba Wild Mules faces Limeira Jetsons (2-1, 327.96).

### Sorocaba Steelers Creeps Past Indaiatuba Riders to Move into 9th Place 

Sorocaba Steelers got 21.40 points from Saquon Barkley (51 Rsh Yds, 43 Rec Yds, 1 TD) and 18.60 from Chase Claypool (96 Rec Yds) to slip by Indaiatuba Riders 93.44 to 91.50. Sorocaba Steelers has been red-hot in this matchup as of late, winning three straight against Indaiatuba Riders. Even though these teams are tied for ninth place with matching 1-2 records, Indaiatuba Riders has a solid lead in points, 345.60 to 271.68. Sorocaba Steelers started one player with zero points while Indaiatuba Riders also served up one goose egg, meaning each team left something on the table.

 There was one player on Indaiatuba Riders that missed out on opportunities. Even though Marquise Brown saw a total of seven targets, he reeled in just three catches.

 Sorocaba Steelers will have to step up their game in Week 4 when they go up against Paulinia Robots (3-0, 436.12), who averages a league-leading 145.37 points per game. Indaiatuba Riders takes on London Knights (0-3, 347.98).

### Limeira Dead Rabbits Finishes Strong Against Indaiatuba Blues, Notches 3rd in a Row 

Down 4.00 on Monday night, Limeira Dead Rabbits came back to defeat Indaiatuba Blues 109.84 to 103.92. Limeira Dead Rabbits picked up 22.70 points from Austin Ekeler (55 Rsh Yds, 52 Rec Yds, 1 Rec TD) and 20.54 from Jalen Hurts (326 Pas Yds, 35 Rsh Yds, 2 TD/2 Int). This matchup included three lead changes, with the largest lead being 28.50 points after Sunday afternoon. Indaiatuba Blues just cannot match up against Limeira Dead Rabbits, who extended their career unbeaten streak to two games in the series. The outcome could have been worse as Limeira Dead Rabbits had a starter score zero points (the Kansas City Chiefs DEF). Limeira Dead Rabbits (3-0, 403.50 points) falls to second place and Indaiatuba Blues (1-2, 357.32 points) drops to seventh place.

 Along with being the one DEF to tally a negative score, the Washington Football Team DEF (on Indaiatuba Blues) represented the only starter in the whole league in negative territory. Sermon had lots of looks for Indaiatuba Blues, but posted just 34 total yards on 12 touches.

 Next week, Limeira Dead Rabbits plays Campinas Giants (2-1, 292.94), while Indaiatuba Blues gets Indaiatuba Pats (2-1, 373.02).
