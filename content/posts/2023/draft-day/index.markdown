## Draft Analysis

### <img src='https://static.www.nfl.com/league/apps/fantasy/logos/avatar/240x240/NYG_2.png' style='width: 50px; height: 50px'/> Rio Claro Farmers

With Top-Tier Ambitions, Rio Claro Farmers Assembles a Solid Ground Game

Rio Claro Farmers had success drafting in the second spot of the opening round, assembling a roster that is capable of reaching the postseason. They stocked up on pass-catching weapons early, using three of their first five selections to pick up wide receivers. Elsewhere on the roster, Rio Claro Farmers was able to land the most prolific threesome of running backs in the league.


### <img src='https://image.fantasy.nfl.com/image/19b9a9b83996bd9277edd5144b2728cb.jpg?x=40&y=27&sig=645537d63e7b1482582fc8153b3860b3' style='width: 50px; height: 50px'/> Paulinia Robots

Projected to Win the League Paulinia Robots Racks Up Wideouts

Paulinia Robots should officially be on the Its Football Dudes radar after taking the seventh overall draft slot and putting together a team that is capable of reaching the postseason. They grabbed wide receivers early, using three of their first five picks to address that position. In conjunction with that drafting strategy, Paulinia Robots ended up with one of the best foursomes of wide receivers in the league.


### <img src='https://image.fantasy.nfl.com/image/2e2e87f937ca70cc5ce0ec49133f1ee1.jpg?x=40&y=27&sig=b9cb87fe7b413b822dd6801effe61f2c' style='width: 50px; height: 50px'/> Goncalves Miners

Although They Stretched for Some Early Picks, Goncalves Miners Looks to Contend for the League's Top Spot

Along with a solid draft position,, Goncalves Miners had an outstanding performance. They didn't overload at any one position early on, instead focusing on balance by drafting a quarterback, running back, wide receiver, and tight end in their first four picks. By draft's end, Goncalves Miners secured the most prolific tandem of tight ends in the league.


### <img src='https://static.www.nfl.com/league/apps/fantasy/logos/avatar/240x240/SF_1.png' style='width: 50px; height: 50px'/> Amparo Bikers

Built on an Amazing Group of Ball Carriers, Amparo Bikers Looks to Factor Heavily into the Playoff Picture

Picking ninth overall in the opening round, Amparo Bikers had a decent showing and delivered a team that will possibly lead to a postseason berth. They were careful to balance their roster from the beginning, acquiring a quarterback, two running backs, and two wide receivers with their first five picks. By the end of the draft, Amparo Bikers landed one of the best foursomes of running backs in the league.


### <img src='https://static.www.nfl.com/league/apps/fantasy/logos/avatar/240x240/NYG_5.png' style='width: 50px; height: 50px'/> Campinas Giants

Campinas Giants Assembles a Ground Attack

When a bottom-half draft slot yields top-half results, that's the sign of a GM with skill. Such was the case with Campinas Giants, which flipped their 10th overall pick into a projected fifth-place finish in Its Football Dudes. They added some running backs early, using three of their first five picks on ball-carriers. Going hand-in-hand with that early investment, Campinas Giants secured the best foursome of running backs in the league.
